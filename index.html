How to Use!

  • Step 1: Mark

    Mark the stainless steel plates with a pen to ensure accuracy.

  • Step 2: Indent

    Using a center punch (automatic punch recommended) indent the steel plate.

  • Step 3: Insert and Store

    Insert Steel backup into Tamper-Evident Deposit Bag provided and store it in your safe place.
